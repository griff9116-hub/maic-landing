The Programme

Six Months to Raise-Ready

Each phase is designed to deepen VC relationships and build the evidence base investors need — so by Month 6, you're already in conversations with the relevant investors, not trying to get in the door via a cold approach.

01.

Month 1

Onboarding & Investor Thesis Alignment

  • Kickoff workshop — investor landscape briefing
  • Deep-dive startup profiling & one-pager creation
  • Map and outreach to 50+ target VCs
  • Personalised Investment Memo per startup
02.

Month 2

Investor Education & First Touchpoints

  • "Marine Autonomy 2026" investor briefing event
  • Facilitated 2–3 startup–VC intros per company
  • Speed-dating VC office hours (2-hour format)
  • Pitch refinement: how to speak to marine VCs
03.

Month 3

Deep Dive & Relationship Building

  • 30-minute deep-dive sessions with small VC groups
  • VC Advisory Board roundtable on sector trends
  • Custom shortlist of 5–10 priority investors per startup
  • Monthly newsletter of startup milestones to 50+ VCs
04.

Month 4

Validation & Soft Circles

  • Steampunk schedules follow-up VC meetings
  • Workshop: how to build a soft circle
  • Startup progress reviews & investor-targeting refinement
  • VC testimonial library built (video/quote format)
05.

Month 5

Pre-Raise Preparation

  • Live pitch practice with a panel of 3–5 VCs
  • Data room setup: what investors actually want
  • Identify 5–7 warm leads with strong intent signals
  • Speed networking — 10-minute 1:1s with matched VCs
06.

Month 6

Commitment & Handoff to Fundraising

  • VC commitment sessions — valuation & round size discussions
  • Final Investor Showcase (Demo Day) to 50+ VCs
  • Personalised 6-month fundraising roadmap
  • Programme retrospective & alumni network access
